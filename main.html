2026.04.24 [Berlin]
- Back in Berlin -

Recently　I've been going through some big changes in my life. I've been feeling old lately... I kinda stopped going out, have been sober and being healthy, and am focusing on making art a lil low-key, working quite a lot to survive. upgraded my gear, made my room cozy, and it brings me so much joy :) Is it cherry blossom season already? I feel like Berlin is such a deconstructive city, it makes everyone reflect on themselves: relationships, jobs, housing, mental health. You either get lost or find out who you want to become.

I'm not sure where I stand yet tho. I'm super ADHD all the time, so many things want to do I have to write down every hour what I need to do, even just drinking water. music, collective, printmaking, art, design, job, nails, they're all like my meditative side quests. This year, I feel like I don't want to compete with anyone anymore. I don't need to try to be cool or whatever, don't need to prove anything to anyone. As long as we do what we want to do and feel fulfilled & that's enough. Feeling super grateful about people surround me u all really support me, no matter who encountered my life, past, now, or future, if our path came across, I believe it's all fate that bring us together & progress :)

2026.03.06–03.29 [Vietnam]
- Hcmc-Mui Ne-Dalat-Da nang-HoiAn-Hanoi-

It was my first time traveling in a communist country, and also only the second country I've been to in Southeast Asia. Such a different experience, the streets are chaotic, and the buildings and people remind me of Taiwan in the 80s: simple, not too gentrified.

I'm glad I got to travel with my closest friends. We were invited to a secret venue in HCMC hidden between skyscrapers, drew on a beach, rode bikes through rice fields, walked through thousands of lanterns, and just kept eating hehe.

Got food poisoning, but also had amazing pho and rolls. Got scammed, drank Saigon beer, went to some boring tech-house events at clubs, shared cigarettes and sweet coffee.

Sometimes I feel privileged traveling here. Even though I'm broke, at least I have options. But at the same time, these cities feel so alive.

2026.03.06–03.29 [Thailand]
- Chiangmai -

Still can't believe how we pulled it off lol. Looking back at Dec + Jan, I think we hosted 15+ events at Ancient Effect. Every day was just contacting people, making flyers, helping set up—nonstop.

I feel so grateful for everyone who trusted us:) Especially all the artist friends who came through—Berlin, Taipei, and different parts of the world we crossed paths with before.

Shoutout to Lilium, Internet Culture, Solar Bear, Chiang Mai Punk Fest, CaveCave, Chiang Mai Original Live. And my event companions Angel, Yee, Damla — I only have good memories.
